CatchKeeper Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03/07/2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to CatchKeeper, a mobile application designed for anglers to record and track their catches. Protecting your privacy is a priority. This privacy policy explains what information is collected, how it is stored, and how it is used.

2. Data Collected

CatchKeeper does not collect any personal data or share information with third parties. All data recorded by the user, including:

  • Photos of catches
  • Fish size and weight
  • Catch date
  • Customized species list (species name, legal catch size)

is stored locally on the userâ€™s device via WatermelonDB.

3. Data Storage and Security

All information entered in the application remains exclusively on your device. No data is sent to remote servers. CatchKeeper does not sync or back up data to the cloud.

4. App Permissions

The application requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • Access to the camera and storage: Used to record and display photos of catches.

None of this data is shared with third parties.

5. Purchase and Monetization

CatchKeeper is a paid application with a one-time payment. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements.

6. User Rights

  • You can delete all your data by uninstalling the application.
  • Since no data is stored online, it is not possible to recover your information after deleting the application.
